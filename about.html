About HeyHelpy

Building trust-powered communities for families

Our Mission

At HeyHelpy, we believe every family deserves access to trusted, reliable help. Our mission is to create safe, connected communities where parents can find quality childcare and educational support with confidence and ease.

Our Vision

In a tech-first world, HeyHelpy keeps human help accessible. We envision neighborhoods where families support each other, backed by technology that enhances trust and convenience.

Our Values

  • Safety First: Comprehensive background checks
  • Community: Building local connections
  • Innovation: AI-powered matching
  • Family: Understanding unique needs

The Story Behind HeyHelpy

HeyHelpy was born from the everyday struggles of busy parents who needed reliable help but didn't know where to turn. Our founders, parents themselves, experienced the frustration of lengthy searches, uncertain qualifications, and lack of local options.

By combining advanced AI matching technology with rigorous safety standards, we've created a platform that makes finding trusted help as simple as ordering a ride. Today, we're proud to serve families nationwide, connecting them with qualified tutors and babysitters in their own neighborhoods.
