About HeyHelpy

Building trust-powered communities for families in North Texas

Our Mission

At HeyHelpy, we believe every family deserves access to trusted, reliable help. Our mission is to create safe, connected communities where parents can find quality childcare and educational support with confidence and ease throughout North Texas.

Our Vision

In a tech-first world, HeyHelpy keeps human help accessible. We envision neighborhoods where families support each other, backed by technology that enhances trust and convenience in North Texas communities.

Our Values

  • Safety First: Comprehensive background checks
  • Community: Building local connections
  • Innovation: AI-powered matching
  • Family: Understanding unique needs
  • Transparency: Clear legal protections

Built on Professional Standards

HeyHelpy is designed from the ground up to be professional and protective of families. We operate as a family-focused platform connecting parents with qualified independent professionals, ensuring clear standards and protections for all users.

Our technology platform connects families with background-verified service providers while maintaining appropriate professional boundaries and compliance with industry best practices.

The Story Behind HeyHelpy

HeyHelpy was born from the everyday struggles of busy parents in North Texas suburbs who needed reliable help but didn't know where to turn. Our founders, parents themselves, experienced the frustration of lengthy searches, uncertain qualifications, and lack of local options.

By combining advanced AI matching technology with rigorous safety standards and legal compliance, we've created a platform that makes finding trusted help as simple as ordering a ride. Now serving North Texas, we're building the future of family support.
