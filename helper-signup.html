Independent Tutoring Professional Information

As an independent tutoring professional on HeyHelpy, you will manage your own tutoring business including:

  • • Setting your own tutoring rates and schedules
  • • Developing personalized lesson plans and teaching methods
  • • Managing your own business taxes and professional insurance
  • • Maintaining any required teaching certifications or licenses
  • • Providing quality educational services to students
  • • Following all applicable educational and professional standards

HeyHelpy provides the technology platform to connect you with families seeking quality tutoring services in their local community.
