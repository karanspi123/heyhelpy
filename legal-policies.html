Legal Policies

Understanding the legal framework for our tutoring platform

HeyHelpy Legal Framework

HeyHelpy connects families with independent tutoring professionals for educational services. You must be 15+ to create an account (18+ to provide tutoring services). Payments are processed securely via Stripe. Tutors are independent contractors, not HeyHelpy employees. Your privacy and data security are protected. Location data is used to match families with nearby qualified tutors.

Key Points At A Glance

  • • Platform connects families with independent tutoring professionals
  • • Minimum age: 15+ for accounts, 18+ for tutoring services
  • • Secure payment processing through Stripe
  • • Tutors are independent contractors with their own teaching methods
  • • Session cancellations within 24 hours may incur fees
  • • Privacy and educational data are protected
  • • Location matching for local tutoring connections

1. Agreement to Terms

Welcome to HeyHelpy! These Legal Policies govern your access to and use of the HeyHelpy mobile application and platform operated by HeyHelpy, Inc. By downloading, accessing, or using our platform, you agree to be bound by these policies in conjunction with our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to these policies, you may not access or use the platform.

2. Educational Services Overview

HeyHelpy is a technology platform that connects families seeking tutoring services ("Parents" or "Students") with independent tutoring professionals who can provide educational services ("Tutors"). Our platform facilitates the connection between families and tutors, and provides tools for session booking, communication, scheduling, academic progress tracking, and secure payment processing through Stripe.

Educational Focus

All services on HeyHelpy are educational in nature, including academic tutoring, test preparation, homework assistance, and learning support across various subjects and grade levels.

3. Account Registration

3.1 Account Creation

To use our platform, you must create an account. When registering, you agree to provide accurate, current, and complete information. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials and for all activities that occur under your account.

3.2 Age Requirements

  • General Users: Must be 15 years or older to create an account
  • Tutors: Must be 18 years or older to provide tutoring services
  • Parent Accounts: Parents may create accounts to find tutoring services for their students
  • Student Protection: We do not collect personal information directly from children under 15

3.3 Account Security

You are solely responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password, restricting access to your account, and notifying us immediately of any unauthorized use of your account. We reserve the right to suspend or terminate your account if we suspect unauthorized or fraudulent use.

4. User Roles and Responsibilities

4.1 Parents/Students (Education Seekers)

As a family seeking tutoring services, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and complete information about educational needs
  • Set reasonable academic expectations and learning goals
  • Communicate clearly with tutors about student progress
  • Pay for completed tutoring sessions promptly
  • Provide honest and fair ratings/reviews of tutoring services
  • Ensure student safety and appropriate learning environments
  • Not request illegal, inappropriate, or non-educational services

4.2 Tutors (Educational Service Providers)

As a tutor offering educational services, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate information about your qualifications and expertise
  • Complete tutoring sessions to the best of your professional ability
  • Communicate effectively with families about student progress
  • Maintain professional conduct and educational standards at all times
  • Report income and pay applicable taxes as required by law
  • Comply with all applicable educational laws and regulations
  • Complete background verification and maintain required credentials
  • Prioritize student safety and educational outcomes

5. Independent Contractor Status

Critical Legal Distinction

Tutors are independent educational contractors, NOT employees of HeyHelpy. This distinction is fundamental to the platform's operation.

Tutors are independent contractors, not employees of HeyHelpy. We do not control, direct, or manage tutors' teaching methods or educational approaches. Tutors determine their own:

  • Teaching methodologies and educational approaches
  • Availability and scheduling preferences
  • Tutoring rates and pricing structure
  • Which students and subjects to accept
  • Educational materials and learning resources

Each tutor is responsible for their own taxes, professional insurance, educational licenses, and compliance with applicable laws.

6. Payment Processing & Financial Terms

6.1 Secure Payment Processing

  • All payments are processed securely through Stripe
  • HeyHelpy does not store credit card information
  • Payment disputes are handled through our platform support
  • Refunds are processed according to our Cancellation Policy

6.2 Platform Fees

  • HeyHelpy charges a service fee for facilitating connections
  • Fees are clearly disclosed before booking confirmation
  • Platform fees support safety verification and technology infrastructure
  • Tutors set their own rates; platform fees are additional

6.3 Tutor Payments

  • Tutors receive payment after session completion
  • Payments are subject to our verification process
  • Tutors are responsible for reporting income for tax purposes
  • Payment schedules and methods are outlined in tutor agreements

7. Safety & Background Verification

Comprehensive Safety Measures

All tutors undergo rigorous background verification including criminal history checks, identity verification, and professional reference validation.

7.1 Required Verifications

  • Criminal background screening through certified providers
  • Identity document verification
  • Professional reference checks
  • Educational credential verification where applicable
  • Ongoing monitoring and review processes

7.2 Limitations of Background Checks

  • Background checks have inherent limitations
  • May not reveal all relevant information
  • Users should exercise additional due diligence
  • HeyHelpy is not liable for undiscovered information

8. Data Protection & Privacy

Your privacy and data security are fundamental to our platform. Our comprehensive data protection measures include:

  • Educational records protection compliance
  • Secure data encryption and storage
  • Limited data collection focused on educational needs
  • Location data used only for tutor-family matching
  • No data sales to third-party marketers

For complete details, please review our Privacy Policy.

9. Intellectual Property

9.1 Platform Content

  • HeyHelpy platform design, technology, and branding are protected
  • Users may not copy, reproduce, or distribute platform content
  • Educational materials created by tutors remain their intellectual property
  • User-generated content (reviews, messages) may be used for platform improvement

9.2 Educational Materials

  • Tutors retain ownership of their teaching materials and methods
  • Families may not redistribute tutor-created educational content
  • Academic progress reports may be shared with parent consent
  • Platform-generated data insights remain HeyHelpy property

10. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited on the HeyHelpy platform:

General Prohibitions

  • Fraudulent or misleading information
  • Harassment or inappropriate conduct
  • Violation of laws or regulations
  • Circumventing platform safety measures
  • Unauthorized access to accounts or data

Educational Specific

  • Misrepresenting educational qualifications
  • Inappropriate content or materials
  • Academic dishonesty or cheating assistance
  • Non-educational service requests
  • Violation of student privacy rights

11. Liability & Insurance

11.1 Platform Liability Limitations

  • HeyHelpy facilitates connections but does not provide educational services
  • We are not liable for tutor actions, teaching methods, or academic outcomes
  • Maximum liability limited to platform fees paid in preceding 12 months
  • No liability for consequential, indirect, or punitive damages

11.2 Insurance Recommendations

  • Tutors should maintain professional liability insurance
  • Families should verify their homeowner's/renter's insurance coverage
  • Additional coverage may be recommended for in-home services
  • HeyHelpy does not provide insurance coverage for users

12. Compliance & Legal Obligations

12.1 Educational Compliance

  • Compliance with educational privacy laws
  • Student records protection requirements
  • Academic integrity standards
  • Special education accommodations where applicable

12.2 Geographic Compliance

  • Platform operates under Texas state law
  • Services provided in North Texas area initially
  • Compliance with local educational regulations
  • Expansion subject to legal requirements in new jurisdictions

12.3 Reporting Obligations

  • Mandatory reporting of safety concerns
  • Cooperation with law enforcement when required
  • Educational standard violations reporting
  • Data breach notification procedures
