Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

HeyHelpy is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform to connect with tutors and babysitters. We only collect data from users 15 years and older and never collect data directly from children.

1. Information We Collect

Adult Users Only (15+)

We only collect personal information from users 15 years and older. We do not collect data directly from children under 15.

Information You Provide:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, bio, qualifications, availability
  • Service Information: Service requests, preferences, special needs
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely)
  • Communication Data: Messages, reviews, ratings, support requests
  • Family Information: Number and ages of children (provided by parents with consent)

Information We Collect Automatically:

  • Usage Data: App usage, session duration, features used
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique identifiers
  • Location Data: Approximate location for matching services (with permission)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, app version
  • Booking Data: Service history, session details, feedback

Third-Party Information:

  • Background Check Data: Criminal history, identity verification (for helpers)
  • Payment Data: Transaction status from payment processors
  • Social Media: Public profile information (if you connect accounts)
  • References: Professional references and recommendations

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Platform Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match you with appropriate helpers/families
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Facilitate communication between users
  • Provide customer support

Safety & Security

  • Conduct background checks
  • Verify user identities
  • Monitor platform safety
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Investigate safety incidents

Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve AI matching algorithms
  • Develop new features
  • Optimize user experience
  • Conduct research (anonymized)

Legal Compliance

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to legal requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect user rights and safety
  • Maintain business records

3. How We Share Your Information

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

HeyHelpy does not sell your personal information to third-party marketers or advertisers.

With Other Users:

  • Profile Visibility: Helper profiles are visible to families searching for services
  • Booking Information: Contact details shared when booking is confirmed
  • Reviews & Ratings: Feedback visible to other platform users
  • Service Details: General information about service requests

With Service Providers:

  • Stripe (Payment Processing): Secure payment transaction data
  • Checkr (Background Checks): Identity and criminal history verification
  • Twilio (Communications): SMS and messaging services
  • AWS (Cloud Storage): Secure data storage and processing
  • Analytics Providers: Anonymized usage data for platform improvement

All service providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

Legal Requirements:

  • Court orders or legal subpoenas
  • Law enforcement investigations
  • Child safety and protection requirements
  • Emergency situations requiring immediate action
  • Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

Business Transfers:

If HeyHelpy is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred. We will notify users via email and app notification of any such change.

4. Data Security & Protection

Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security audits and testing
  • Secure cloud infrastructure (AWS)
  • Multi-factor authentication options

Access Controls

  • Limited employee access to personal data
  • Regular access reviews and updates
  • Secure authentication for all systems
  • Privacy training for all team members
  • Incident response procedures

Important: While we implement robust security measures, no system is completely secure. You must also protect your account by using strong passwords and not sharing login credentials.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill legal obligations:

Active Accounts

Data retained while account is active and for reasonable period after deactivation

Transaction Records

Financial records kept for 7 years for tax and legal compliance

Safety Records

Background checks and safety incidents retained per legal requirements

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

Access Your Data

Request a copy of the personal information we have about you

Correct Your Data

Update or correct inaccurate personal information

Delete Your Data

Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)

Data Portability

Export your data in a commonly used format

Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your personal information

Withdraw Consent

Opt out of data processing based on consent

To exercise these rights: Contact us at info@heyhelpy.com or through the app settings. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy Protection

COPPA Compliance

HeyHelpy does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 15 years of age.

  • Age Verification: All account holders must be 15 years or older
  • Parental Control: Parents control all information about their children
  • No Direct Collection: We never collect data directly from children
  • Immediate Deletion: Any inadvertently collected child data is deleted immediately
  • Parental Rights: Parents can request deletion of any information about their children

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 15, please contact us immediately at info@heyhelpy.com.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand usage patterns and improve the platform

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and preferences for better experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

10. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information:

  • We will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery
  • Notifications will be sent via email and in-app alert
  • We will provide clear information about what data was affected
  • Immediate steps will be taken to secure the breach
  • We will cooperate fully with regulatory authorities
  • Credit monitoring may be provided if financial data is involved

11. International Data Transfers

HeyHelpy primarily operates in the United States. If you access our platform from other countries:

  • Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States
  • We comply with applicable international data protection laws
  • Appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers
  • You consent to such transfers by using our platform

12. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements:

  • Material changes will be communicated via email and in-app notification
  • Updates will be posted on our website with the effective date
  • Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of updates
  • You can review the full history of changes upon request
