Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

Professional Platform

HeyHelpy is designed for responsible adults to coordinate family services. Only users 15 years and older may create accounts. We do not collect personal information from children under 15, as parents manage all account activities.

Data We Collect

  • Adult user account information (name, email, phone)
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe)
  • Session booking and scheduling data
  • Academic progress notes (educational purposes only)
  • Helper verification and background check results
  • Device information and usage analytics
  • Location data (only with permission, for matching purposes)

Data We Don't Collect

  • Personal information from children under 15
  • Social media profiles of minors
  • Location tracking of children
  • Behavioral data about children
  • Unnecessary personal data not related to our services

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide platform technology services
  • Match families with appropriate helpers
  • Process payments and bookings
  • Conduct background verification
  • Communicate about services and safety
  • Improve our platform and user experience

Data Sharing

We share limited data only when necessary:

  • With helpers and families to facilitate bookings
  • With background check providers for verification
  • With payment processors for transactions
  • With law enforcement when required by law
  • We do not sell personal data to third parties

Independent Contractor Relationship

All helpers are independent contractors. HeyHelpy provides platform technology only and does not employ helpers or provide childcare services. Helpers are responsible for their own services, licensing, and insurance.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Request data portability

Texas Privacy Rights

As a Texas-based company serving Texas residents, we comply with applicable state privacy laws and provide additional protections for our users' personal information.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at:
